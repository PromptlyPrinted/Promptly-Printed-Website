# CAMPAIGN BRIEF: Halloween 2025

## Campaign Overview
- **Campaign Name:** Spook & Style 2025
- **Duration:** September 25, 2025 - November 3, 2025
- **Launch Date:** October 1, 2025
- **Peak Period:** October 15-31, 2025

## Campaign Objectives
### Primary Goals
- **Revenue Target:** Increase Halloween-themed apparel sales by 45% compared to 2024
- **Customer Acquisition:** Generate 1,200 new customer sign-ups during campaign period
- **Brand Awareness:** Establish Promptly Printed as the go-to destination for custom Halloween apparel in the UK market
- **Market Positioning:** Position as the premium, AI-powered custom Halloween clothing solution

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total campaign revenue: £85,000 target
- New customer acquisition: 1,200 customers
- Email list growth: 2,500 new subscribers
- Social media engagement increase: 200%
- Average order value increase: 25%
- Customer retention rate: 65% post-campaign

## Target Audience Analysis

### Primary Audience: Creative Halloween Enthusiasts (18-35)
- **Demographics:** UK-based young adults, 60% female, 40% male
- **Psychographics:**
  - Value unique self-expression and standing out from the crowd
  - Early adopters of technology and AI tools
  - Active on social media (Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest)
  - Disposable income £300-£800/month for lifestyle purchases
- **Behavior Patterns:**
  - Start Halloween shopping in early-mid September ("Summerween" trend)
  - Prefer personalized over mass-produced items
  - Influenced by pop culture and viral trends
  - Share costume ideas and purchases on social media

### Secondary Audience: Family Coordinators (25-45)
- **Demographics:** Parents and group organizers planning coordinated costumes
- **Pain Points:** Finding matching, high-quality custom apparel for multiple people
- **Budget:** Higher spend per transaction (£150-£400 for family/group orders)

### Tertiary Audience: Last-Minute Shoppers (22-40)
- **Behavior:** Purchase 1-2 weeks before Halloween
- **Motivation:** Convenience and fast delivery
- **Opportunity:** Premium pricing for express production
