## Phase 4: Posting Strategy

### Optimal Posting Schedule

**Best Times to Post (US audience):**
- **Tuesday-Thursday**: 9-11 AM, 3-5 PM, 8-10 PM EST
- **Friday-Saturday**: 12-2 PM, 7-11 PM EST
- **Sunday**: 7-9 AM, 4-6 PM EST
- **Monday**: Avoid (lowest engagement)

**Posting Frequency:**
- **Week 1-2**: 1 post/day (build consistency)
- **Week 3-4**: 2 posts/day (algorithm favors active accounts)
- **Month 2+**: 2-3 posts/day (high-growth phase)
- **Established**: 3-5 posts/day (maintain momentum)

**Content Calendar Structure:**
```
Monday: Behind-the-Scenes
Tuesday: Product Showcase
Wednesday: Educational Content
Thursday: Trending/Viral Attempt
Friday: Customer Feature
Saturday: Product Showcase #2
Sunday: Community Engagement
```

### Video Specs & Best Practices

**Technical Requirements:**
- **Duration**: 7-60 seconds (sweet spot: 15-30s)
- **Format**: Vertical (9:16 ratio)
- **Resolution**: 1080x1920px minimum
- **Frame rate**: 30fps or 60fps
- **File format**: MP4 or MOV
- **File size**: Under 287.6MB

**Quality Checklist:**
- ‚úÖ Shot in good lighting (natural light best)
- ‚úÖ Stable footage (use tripod or stabilizer)
- ‚úÖ Clear audio (if voiceover)
- ‚úÖ High contrast and vibrant colors
- ‚úÖ Text readable on mobile screens
- ‚úÖ Captions/subtitles (80% watch muted)

**Hook Guidelines:**
- First 1-2 seconds are CRITICAL
- Pattern interrupt (unexpected visual/text)
- Ask a question
- Make a bold claim
- Show something satisfying
- Create curiosity gap

**CTA Best Practices:**
- End every video with action
- "Link in bio"
- "Follow for more [specific benefit]"
- "Comment [word] for [thing]"
- "Save this for later"
- "Share with someone who needs this"

## Red Flags to Avoid

### Don't Do These (Seriously)

**‚ùå Buying followers/views**
- Algorithm detects and suppresses
- Fake engagement = no sales
- Waste of money

**‚ùå Posting inconsistently**
- Algorithm favors active accounts
- Followers forget about you
- Momentum dies

**‚ùå Ignoring comments**
- Engagement signals to algorithm
- Missed community building
- Lost sales opportunities

**‚ùå Only posting sales content**
- People hate ads
- Unfollow rate increases
- No organic reach

**‚ùå Using banned music/sounds**
- Video gets zero reach
- Check "commercial use" before using

**‚ùå Copying competitors exactly**
- Algorithm detects duplicate content
- No differentiation
- Looks unprofessional

**‚ùå Neglecting video quality**
- Poor lighting, shaky footage, bad audio
- Signals low quality brand
- People scroll past immediately

**‚ùå Spamming hashtags**
- 30 hashtags looks desperate
- Use 4-6 relevant ones only
- Quality over quantity
