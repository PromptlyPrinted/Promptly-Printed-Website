### Logo Treatment
- **Primary Logo:** Standard Promptly Printed logo with Halloween color overlay
- **Halloween Variant:** Logo with subtle pumpkin or ghost icon integration
- **Monogram Version:** "PP" initials with Halloween styling for small applications

### Photography Style & Direction

**Lighting:**
- High contrast with dramatic shadows
- Backlighting to create silhouette effects
- Color temperature warm (2700K-3200K) for cozy autumn feel
- Accent lighting in brand purple and orange

**Composition:**
- Rule of thirds with subjects slightly off-center
- Dynamic angles for energy and movement
- Close-ups showing fabric texture and print quality
- Wide shots for lifestyle and group coordination

**Model Direction:**
- Confident, playful poses
- Genuine expressions of joy and creativity
- Diverse representation across age, ethnicity, body types
- Mix of individual and group shots for family market

**Props & Styling:**
- Autumn leaves, pumpkins, lanterns as background elements
- Modern, minimalist Halloween decor (avoid overly scary)
- Tech elements (tablet/phone) showing AI design process
- Sustainable materials visible (eco-friendly packaging, etc.)
